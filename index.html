Progressive Media Solutions

I am an Environmental Videographer and Video Editor. Additionally, I work as a director of photography, camera grip, audio recorder, and photographer. My work in the film industry has brought me all over the Midwest doing documentary, feature films, video journalism, sports broadcasting, and promotional work for local and corperate businesses.
Select Clients: Discover Mediaworks, UW-Psychology, The Badger Herald, The Big Ten Network, Democratic Party of Wisconsin, and Bookworm Gardens.

About Me

Based out of Madison, Wisconsin, I am an experienced videograper with degrees in both Digital Studies and Environmental Sciences from UW-Madison. I am passionate about any work the involves capturing a story with a camera, and telling that story in post production. Seeing my images come to life with the help of coloring and sound design gets me particularly excited.

With the combination of my digital skills and my passion for people and the environment we live in, I intend to leave my mark on the world by spreading knowledge about social and environmental issues through videography and photography.
