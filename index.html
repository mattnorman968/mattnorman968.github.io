Progressive Media Solutions

I am an Environmental Videographer, Editor, and Colorist. Additionally, I work as a director of photography, camera grip, audio recorder, and photographer. My work in the film industry has taken me all over the Midwest filming documentaries, feature films, video journalism pieces, sports broadcasts, and promotional work for local and corporate entities.
Select Clients: Discover Mediaworks, UW-Psychology, The Badger Herald, The Big Ten Network, Democratic Party of Wisconsin, and Bookworm Gardens.

About Me

Based in Madison, Wisconsin, I am an experienced videograper with degrees in both Digital Studies and Environmental Sciences from UW-Madison. I am passionate about any work that involves telling a story with a camera and post-production editing. My ability to bring images to life with the use of coloring and sound design also gives me a great deal of pride.

With the combination of my digital skills and my passion for people and the environment, I produce impactful videography and photography that bring knowledge of social and environmental issues to diverse groups of people.
